About CryoTek Systems

We're passionate about bringing electronics back to life. Whether it's a gaming console, computer, laptop, or another device, we focus on quality repairs and honest service.

🔧 What We Do

We repair desktops, laptops, gaming consoles, handheld systems, perform HDMI port replacements, microsoldering, software repair, hardware upgrades, and more.

⭐ Our Goal

Our goal is to provide reliable repairs, fair pricing, and excellent customer service while helping customers get the most life out of their devices.

🛠 Quality Parts

We source replacement parts from trusted suppliers such as iFixit and other reputable vendors whenever possible.

📦 Mail-In Repairs

Not local? No problem. Our mail-in repair service allows customers to ship their devices to us from anywhere in the United States.
